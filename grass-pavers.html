Grass Pavers

  • Product: Grass Pavers
  • Product Code: UCP-GP
  • Varities: Four Varities

Grass Pavers though works same purpose as interlocking pavers, but they give beautiful grassy coating to the floor. Their honeycomb-design allows soil and gravel and thus eventually provide grass to grow giving your outdoor floor, walkways or pathways a nice & attractive green look.

Moreover the rain water goes to the soil instead of drainage system and hence recharges earth's water table. Hence they are Eco-Friendly too.

About Us

Unique Concrete Prefab is a leader in manufacturing and supplying of concrete products. From interlocking pavers, Chequered Tiles, Wall Claddings, Manhole Covers, Drain Cover, Grass Pavers, Kerb Stone to garden furnitures, RCC furnitures, Spun Pipes We manufacture all in our own manufacturing units.

Our Products are designed with supreme grade raw material and advanced technology, and hence are flawless in terms of finish, quality, shine, strength and durablity. We respect all the standard sizes and practices and keep them in mind while producing the finished products.

It is our mission to offer innovative solutions for all growing demands of construction needs.

We are a team of highly commited and motivated staff headed by our highly experienced Directors
Mr. Rakesh Gaur & Mr. Ravinder Gaur.

The company was started in 1995 for offering distinguished development works of Multi Storey Apartments and manufacturing of finest quality Concrete Products.

Since its inception Unique Concrete Prefab has come a long way. And in its over 19 years of establishment has worked with hundreds of clients with some big brands like
Ansal Api, DLF, Omaxe, BPTP etc.

RAVINDERA CONSTRUCTION CO. is a sister concern of UNIQUE CONCRETE PREFAB.

  • Uttar Pradesh : Lucknow ,Agra, Ghaziabad, Meerut
  • Haryana : Gurgaon, Sonepat, Panipat, Kurukshetra, Faridabad
